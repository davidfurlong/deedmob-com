Impact Examples From Our Partners

Corbion

Corbion was recently awarded the Ecovadis Platinum Sustainability Rating, being the top 1% in its sector, assessed worldwide. Attributing specifically to Sustainable Development Goals 2, 3, and 12, Corbion’s platform empowers employees to start initiatives and join initiatives that contribute to these goals worldwide. The platform is available in 6 languages and is an integral part of Corbion’s CSR strategy.

TomTom

TomTom’s employees contribute over a 1.000 hours yearly on volunteering via Deedmob, making the company exemplary of corporate volunteering. Hosting volunteer weeks, CEO for a day, Girl’s week, and World Cleanup Day, TomTom continues to make an impact, partnering with Deedmob. Besides these activities, TomTom hosts Codam, a free programming course, taught by TomTom employees, making programming accessible to everyone!
