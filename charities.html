Impact Examples From Our Partners

10.000 HOURS Foundation

10,000 HOURS Foundation was born out of the music industry giant ID&T. Through the foundation, volunteers organize mini festivals like Mystery Land and Defqon to give people who cannot go to festivals, due to a disability for example, a great time. The DJs of the big festivals come to play. 

KidsRights

International NGO KidsRights is one of the champions in online volunteering. Their platform State of Youth helps young people around the world organise their own initiatives, such as a girl in India who is leading a project to plant 5,000 trees, and a girl in Indonesia who is raising awareness about the problem of violence against women. The platform empowers them to get the most out of their capabilities and gives them a podium to share their experiences and contributions with the world.
